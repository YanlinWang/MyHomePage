About me

I received the B.S. degree in computer science from Zhejiang University in 2014. In July 2019, I received the PhD degree in computer science from the University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Bruno C. d. S. Oliveira. In September 2019, I joined the software analytics group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).
